About Ahoi.

"Ahoi!" - Alexander Graham Bell

Alexander Graham Bell once envisioned that "Ahoi!" would be the standard greeting when answering the phone. Inspired by the merging of the Internet and telephone technology, the founders of Ahoi developed the first communication APIs in the early 2000s. Their pioneering work laid the foundation for modern communication tools, and they went on to hold leadership roles at major companies in the industry.

Our people didn’t just innovate technology—they fostered a culture of innovation that has shaped the communication landscape. The company's commitment to pushing boundaries and embracing forward-thinking ideas has cemented its reputation as a trailblazer, continuously inspiring others to build the next generation of groundbreaking solutions.

features.

Here are just some of the 100s of features our system provides:

Global Phone Numbers

Ahoi provides global phone numbers that allow businesses to establish a local presence anywhere in the world. With Ahoi, you can purchase, manage, and integrate phone numbers for voice and SMS communication, helping you connect with customers worldwide.

Global Messaging

Ahoi offers global messaging solutions that enable businesses to send and receive SMS, MMS, and chat messages from anywhere. With reliable and scalable APIs, Ahoi ensures seamless communication with your customers, supporting campaigns and notifications in real-time.

Virtual Machines

Ahoi's Virtual Machines provide powerful, flexible, and secure computing resources. Whether you need infrastructure for hosting applications or processing large amounts of data, Ahoi's VMs are designed to scale with your business needs, offering high availability and performance.

Visual Designer

Ahoi's Visual Designer allows users to create communication workflows with a simple drag-and-drop interface. Without needing to write code, businesses can build automated call flows, SMS sequences, and customer interactions, streamlining their communication processes.

White Label

Ahoi offers White Label solutions that allow businesses to rebrand and resell Ahoi's communication services under their own brand. From SMS and voice services to full API access, Ahoi provides the backend infrastructure, while you manage the customer-facing experience.

Storage

Ahoi provides secure and scalable cloud storage, ensuring your data is always accessible and protected. Whether for storing voice recordings, messages, or application data, Ahoi's storage solutions are optimized for seamless integration with your communication services.

Web RTC

Ahoi leverages WebRTC technology to enable real-time communication through voice and video directly in the browser. With Ahoi's WebRTC solutions, you can integrate high-quality video calls, live chat, and real-time collaboration tools into your applications.

Soft Phone app

Ahoi's Softphone App allows businesses to make and receive calls over the internet without traditional phone hardware. With support for voice, SMS, and chat, Ahoi's softphone solution is perfect for remote teams and businesses that need flexible communication tools.

SIP Phones

Ahoi provides full support for SIP phones, enabling businesses to deploy voice-over-IP services using the latest SIP technology. With Ahoi, you can manage multiple SIP devices for efficient, cost-effective communication.

SMS Chat Client

Ahoi's SMS Chat Client lets businesses engage in two-way messaging with customers through SMS. The platform allows seamless real-time communication, helping businesses respond quickly to inquiries and automate customer support workflows.

SMS Bulk Sending

Ahoi's Bulk Sending service enables businesses to send high-volume text messages with ease. Whether for marketing campaigns, alerts, or notifications, Ahoi offers reliable, scalable infrastructure to ensure your messages reach your audience quickly and securely.

Fax Services

Ahoi provides Fax Services that bring traditional faxing into the digital age. Businesses can send and receive faxes through the cloud, integrating this capability into their communication workflows without the need for fax machines. Have a real Fax Machine? We offer Fax ATAs that provide 100% reliability for the standard Fax machine.

Voice Mail

Ahoi's Voicemail service ensures that no call goes unanswered. With customizable greetings and message storage, Ahoi provides businesses with an easy way to manage voicemails and ensure consistent customer communication.

Advance Call Flow

Ahoi's dvanced Call Flows enable businesses to design and manage sophisticated call routing paths. From IVRs to automated call handling, Ahoi's call flow tools ensure that your customers are directed to the right place every time.

MultiLevel Billing

Ahoi offers MultiLevel Billing solutions that allow businesses to manage and automate billing processes across multiple tiers. This is especially useful for resellers and white-label partners who need flexible billing configurations.

Call Recording

Ahoi provides Call Recording services that allow businesses to capture and store voice calls for quality assurance, training, and compliance purposes. With Ahoi, recordings are stored securely and accessible via the cloud.
